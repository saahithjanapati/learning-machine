# AI For Art

Path: `topics/ai/ai-for-art`

## Scope

This AI subtopic collects lessons about generative and assistive AI systems for creative work.

Use it for material about:

- image, video, music, dance, design, and multimodal art generation;
- artist-facing tools, creative workflows, and co-creation interfaces;
- controllability, style, taste, evaluation, authorship, and cultural impact;
- technical papers where the main contribution is an artistic or creative medium.
- foundation papers when they directly explain the media tokenization, compression, or representation layer used by creative AI systems.

Keep broad model-scaling, alignment, agent, and robotics notes in the root `topics/ai` collection unless the creative/artistic application is central.
