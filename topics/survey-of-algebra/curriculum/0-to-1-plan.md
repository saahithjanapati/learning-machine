## Transcript Anchors

- Foundations: commutative rings, fields, ordered rings, and arithmetic axioms.
- Number theory layer: divisibility, greatest common divisors, Euclidean algorithm, congruences, and modular arithmetic.
- Group theory layer: group axioms, subgroups, cyclic groups, isomorphisms, symmetric groups, and dihedral-style examples.
- Ring theory layer: rings, units, zero divisors, domains, and polynomial rings over commutative rings or fields.
- Source-folder extras: finite Markov chains, continuous-time Markov chains, martingales, and Brownian motion lectures that were present in the same local folder.
