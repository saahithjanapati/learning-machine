## Calibration Notes

This mock is intentionally tuned to feel like `Exam 1`, not like a homework set.

That means:

- the `True / False` section is mostly conceptual trap-checking
- the `Select All` section mixes precise theorem statements with small logical traps
- the `Short Answer / Proof` section is kept in the `6-10 line` exam style rather than full homework-scale derivations

In particular, I tried to keep the difficulty in the same band as Exam 1:

- broad conceptual coverage
- moderate formal precision
- short proofs or derivations, not long calculations
