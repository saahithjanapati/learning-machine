# Bhagavad Gita Core Curriculum

This subject is a gentle first pass through the Bhagavad Gita. It is designed for a reader who wants to know what happens, what the major ideas mean, and why the text has been interpreted in so many ways.

## Big Questions

- What does it mean to do the right thing when every option feels painful?
- How can action be spiritual instead of ego-driven?
- What is the relation between self-knowledge, devotion, discipline, and ordinary life?
- Why does Krishna teach several paths instead of one simple formula?
- How should we read a sacred text that is both philosophical and dramatic?
